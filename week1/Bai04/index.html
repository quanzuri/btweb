Welcome

At Diamond Health Club, you can stay healthy year-round and have fun doing it! We offer something for everyone. Our state-of-the-art facilities can challenge the most seasoned athlete, while remaining friendly to our first-time users. Be sure to check out our great classes for everyone from children, teens, and adults and seniors. No matter who you are, DHC offers a class for you.

DHC also provides several different membership options. You can register as an individual or a family. We also provide special couples plans. Planning to visit Seattle a few days, weeks, or a month? Our great temporary memberships are tailored to meet the needs of any visitor. Temporary memberships also make great Christmas gifts.

Facilities

  • 2 workout rooms
  • Olympic size pool with at least 3 lanes always open
  • Warm, 3-foot deep therapeutic pool
  • 2 gymnasiums with full size basketball courts
  • Five exercise rooms for private and class instruction
  • Climbing gym
  • 3 racquetball courts
  • On-site child care
